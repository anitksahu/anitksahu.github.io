Welcome

Hi! and welcome to my website. My name is Anit Kumar Sahu. I am a Machine Learning Research Scientist at the Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence, Pittsburgh. Previously, I was a PhD Candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Carnegie Mellon University until November 2018. I defended my PhD in November 2018 [Thesis]

I was lucky to have been advised by Prof. Soummya Kar. Before joining CMU, I spent 5 beautiful years and simultaneously earned my B.Tech and M.Tech degrees in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology,Kharagpur. I worked with Prof. Mrityunjoy Chakraborty during my undergraduate days in IIT Kharagpur.
